4.2 Location Information

Roamie may request access to your location while you are using the app.

If you grant location permission, Roamie may collect or process location information such as:

  • Your current location.
  • Approximate or precise location, depending on your iOS settings.
  • The area, city, neighborhood, or geographic region associated with your current location.
  • A derived area identifier or compatibility-related location representation.
  • Nearby place information based on your current area.
  • Location-derived results, such as nearby place recommendations or area compatibility scores.

Roamie uses location information to provide features such as:

  • Showing nearby places.
  • Comparing nearby places with your saved home area.
  • Displaying area compatibility scores.
  • Helping you understand how your current area relates to your selected or saved areas.
  • Supporting Apple Watch companion features that show nearby or home-area related information.

For example, Roamie may use your current area to display nearby place recommendations and compare them with your saved home area to show an area compatibility score.

Roamie requests location access only when it is needed for location-based features. If you do not grant location permission, some location-based features may not work, but Roamie may provide alternative flows such as manually selecting a home area or area of interest where available.

Roamie does not require location permission for every possible app experience. However, certain features that depend on nearby places or your current area may require location access to function properly.

6. Location Permission and User Control

Roamie asks for location permission through the iOS system permission dialog.

You control whether Roamie can access your location. You can grant or deny permission when prompted, and you can change your choice later in your device settings.

On iOS, you can manage location permission by going to:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Roamie

Depending on your device settings, you may be able to choose whether Roamie can access:

  • No location.
  • Approximate location.
  • Precise location.
  • Location only while using the app.

Roamie's location-based features may work differently depending on your permission settings.

If you deny or restrict location permission, Roamie may provide a fallback experience, such as manually selecting a home area or area of interest where available.

Roamie does not require users to grant location permission for unrelated purposes. Location permission is requested to support app features such as nearby places, home area comparison, and area compatibility scores.
